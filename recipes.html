Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make my waffles extra crispy?
For extra crispy waffles, separate your eggs and beat the whites until stiff peaks form. Fold them into the batter at the end. Also, cook them a bit longer than usual and let them rest on a wire rack instead of stacking them.
Can I make waffle batter ahead of time?
Yes! You can make waffle batter up to 2 days ahead. Store it in the refrigerator and give it a gentle stir before using. The batter may thicken slightly, so you might need to add a tablespoon of milk.
What's the secret to fluffy waffles?
The key is not overmixing your batter. Mix just until the ingredients are combined - lumps are okay! Also, make sure your waffle iron is properly preheated and use room temperature ingredients for better mixing.
How do I store leftover waffles?
Cool waffles completely, then store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days, or freeze them for up to 3 months. Toast them directly from frozen for best results.
